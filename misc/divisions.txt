The 14-game schedule in the ten-team divisional era (2008-2011) was:
* One game against all other teams (nine games)
* Another game against all teams in your division (four games)
* Another game against your designated rival from the other division (one game)
* The final game of the regular season was always against your designated rival

The designated cross-divisional rivalries were:
* Daniel vs. Trevor
* John vs. Syed
* Keyon vs. James
* Patrick vs. Koci
* Schexes vs. Jeff & Jake/Luke
